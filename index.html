How I Work

From first message to production-ready.

A simple, repeatable process that keeps projects on time and clients informed at every stage.

01
Discovery

Understand your business, constraints, and goals. Define scope, timeline, and what success actually looks like before writing a line of code.

02
Architecture

Design the system before building it. Stack selection, data flow, integration map, and a clear handoff plan — so nothing is ambiguous mid-build.

03
Build

Ship in focused sprints with regular check-ins. Clean code, tests, and documentation included — not bolted on at the end.

04
Handoff

Deployment, walkthrough, and 30 days of post-launch support. No ghosting after go-live. You know how it works and how to maintain it.
